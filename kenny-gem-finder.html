Product Demo

AI-Powered Search Results

Kenny analyzes 100+ sources including Reddit discussions, expert reviews, and user reports to surface quality products. Results are organized into Good/Better/Best value tiers, helping users make informed decisions based on durability and total cost of ownership rather than marketing hype.

Value-Based Recommendations

Each product recommendation includes cost-per-year analysis, durability ratings, and real user feedback. The platform cuts through marketing noise to show actual product longevity and value, helping consumers avoid planned obsolescence and save money long-term.

Key Features

Multi-Agent Research Pipeline

Agentic architecture coordinates multiple AI agents to gather comprehensive product data from diverse sources, ensuring thorough and unbiased analysis.

100+ Sources Per Search

Analyzes Reddit discussions, expert reviews, user reports, and more to provide a complete picture of product quality and longevity.

Good/Better/Best Tiers

Uses value-based pricing framework to categorize products, making it easy to find the right quality level for your budget and needs.

Cost-Per-Year Analysis

Shows total cost of ownership over time, not just sticker price, revealing which products offer true long-term value.

The Problem

Consumers waste billions on low-quality products that fail prematurely due to planned obsolescence. Traditional product search prioritizes paid ads and marketing over genuine quality. Kenny Gem Finder solves this by analyzing real user experiences and durability data to surface products that last, helping consumers make sustainable purchasing decisions and save money long-term.
