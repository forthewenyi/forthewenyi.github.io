CareerFit AI

Chrome extension that helps job seekers evaluate job fit using AI, providing intelligent analysis and automation for the job search process.

View on GitHub
Chrome Extension Gemini 2.5 Flash Firebase JavaScript Zod

Extension Overview

Chrome Extension Integration

CareerFit AI seamlessly integrates into your job search workflow as a Chrome extension. Install it once and get intelligent job analysis on every job posting you visit across LinkedIn, Indeed, and other job boards.

Core Features

Quick Action Buttons

Access key features directly from any job posting with a draggable button bar. Get job summaries, assess fit scores, auto-fill applications, and track your job search, all without leaving the page.

AI-Powered Job Scoring

Get instant fit scores (1-5 scale) for any job posting. CareerFit AI analyzes the job description against your resume to identify how well you match, highlighting your strengths and any potential gaps.

Smart Analysis & Tracking

Smart Gap Detection

Discover exactly what skills or experience you're missing for each role. The AI provides specific, actionable recommendations with learning resources to help you bridge the gap and become a stronger candidate.

5-Tab Job Dashboard

Track your entire job search in one place with tabs for All Jobs, Interested, Applied, To Learn, and Learned. Easily move jobs through your pipeline and keep your applications organized.

Skills Learning Tracker

Save skills you need to learn from job assessments. Track your progress with recommended resources, add keywords to your resume, and mark skills as learned when you have acquired them. Turn job rejections into learning opportunities.

Key Features

AI Job Scoring (1-5 Scale)

Instant fit analysis comparing your resume to job requirements, with color-coded scores to quickly identify your best opportunities.

Gap Detection & Resources

Identify missing skills with smart recommendations and curated learning resources to help you become a stronger candidate.

Application Auto-Fill

Automatically fills common ATS questions on Workday, Greenhouse, and Lever platforms, saving hours of repetitive data entry.

Resume Bullet Matching

Get AI suggestions on which resume bullets to highlight for each specific job, showing how your experience aligns with role requirements.

The Problem

Job seekers waste countless hours applying to positions where they are not a good fit, leading to rejection fatigue and missed opportunities. Traditional job search tools do not provide personalized fit analysis or help candidates understand what skills they need to develop. CareerFit AI solves this by providing instant, AI-powered job fit scores, identifying skill gaps, and tracking learning progress to turn every job search into a strategic career development opportunity.
